Chapter 2 | The Test

One day while Robert was in the school hallway, the biggest bully at school, Frank, came up to Robert and pushed him down and said, "you are nothing but a weak little mouse." Everybody was looking on. At that moment, he remembered what his mom had told him before, "God has made you special, and you will do great things." He then stood up with confidence and said, "I may be small, but I am really fast." "Not faster than me", said Frank. Robert then replied with confidence, "we will see come recess time." "You're on", said Frank. Everybody left with anticipation for recess to see if Robert could beat the biggest bully in the school in a race.

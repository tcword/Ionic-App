Chapter 3 | The Race

When the recess bell rang, everybody ran to playground and encircled both Robert and Frank. "Here I am", said Frank. "I'm about to show everybody that you are nothing but a weak little mouse." Robert grinned and said, "we'll see about that." They lined up behind the starting line and dug their feet into the ground. Mincy said, "on your mark, get set, GO!" They both took off! Robert blasted off the finish line like a rocket! It was so forceful that it created a cloud of smoke so great that they couldn't even see Frank. When the smoke settled, Frank was still running, while Robert was on the other side of the finish line looking at his watch and yawning. Everyone started to laugh. As Frank crossed the finish line, he looked at Robert in total shock. "What happened?" Robert looked at Frank and said, "Everyone is different Frank. You may be big and strong, but I am small and fast. We need to value one anothers differences and know that we are all special in our own way." And from that day forward, Frank the bully never picked on anyone again, and everyone saw Robert as a hero because of the day he stood up to Frank and taught him a lesson


The End
